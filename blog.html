💻 My Journey Learning Web Development

When I first started learning web development, I honestly did not know how to create a website at all. Everything looked complicated, and I thought coding would be very difficult to understand.

With guidance from my lecturers, countless tutorials on YouTube, and regular practice, I slowly became more confident in my skills. As I continued learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I realized that web development was not as difficult as I had imagined.

One of my favorite things about web development is the ability to customize a website exactly the way I want. I enjoy choosing colors, layouts, and designs that match my personality. Since pink is my favorite color, I often incorporate pink themes into my projects to make them feel more personal and creative.

🎬 Creating My 3D Animation Project

One of the most memorable projects I worked on was creating a 3D animation using Blender. For this project, we were required to create a character based on our own face and ensure that the model could be properly rigged for animation.

The project was challenging because it involved many different stages, including character modeling, texturing, rigging, animation, lighting, and rendering. Throughout the project, I encountered many difficulties and technical issues that required patience and persistence to solve.

Despite the challenges, I successfully completed the animation and gained valuable experience in 3D modeling, problem-solving, and creative design. This project taught me the importance of perseverance and continuous learning when working on complex multimedia projects.

📸 My Photography Journey

I began learning photography during Semester 3, but my interest in photography started much earlier. Growing up, I was always surrounded by cameras because my father is a photographer.

Watching him work inspired me to become interested in capturing moments and telling stories through photographs. As I learned more about photography, I discovered that it involves much more than simply taking pictures.

Through photography, I learned about composition, lighting, camera settings, and photo editing techniques. It has become one of my favorite hobbies because it allows me to express my creativity while preserving meaningful memories.
