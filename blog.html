🌐 What is a Smart Campus Network?

A smart campus network integrates networking technologies, IoT devices, cloud services, and communication systems to improve campus operations and user experiences.

Smart campuses allow students, lecturers, and administrators to access digital services efficiently while improving security, connectivity, and resource management.

🔒 Benefits of VLAN Segmentation

VLAN segmentation divides a network into smaller logical groups, improving network security, performance, and management efficiency.

By separating users and services into different VLANs, organizations can reduce unnecessary traffic and strengthen security controls across the network.

🚀 Introduction to IPv6

IPv6 was developed to overcome the limitations of IPv4 by providing a significantly larger address space for modern internet-connected devices.

As technology continues to grow, IPv6 plays an important role in supporting IoT devices, cloud services, and future internet expansion.
